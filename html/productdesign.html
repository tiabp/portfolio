Product Design
LANDR is the creative platform for musicians. LANDR offers AI-powered music mastering, distribution, plugins, collaboration, promotion and sample packs.
Project
Create an account settings flow that promotes user autonomy and easy access to account and subscription information.
Process
Current setup requires users to reach out to support to change their email. To alleviate this step we wanted to introduce editable text boxes for the user details area. As for the plans section, details were missing as to what was included with each subscription purchased. We were interested in having plan descriptions for users to have direct knowledge to their plan details without too many clicks.
